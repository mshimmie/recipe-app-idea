Cloud Sync

When you save a recipe with Chef it, it is saved in the cloud and synced with all your devices. Don't have access to the cloud? Download the recipe to your phone and make it accessible even without internet or 4G.

Make it

No more standing with the fridge door open, wondering what you can make with a blank stare. With Chef it, list the ingredients you have and find a list of recipes that have the most match to your listed ingredients.

Now it's easy to get your friend's ooey-gooey chocolate chip recipe or learn your mom's secret to her Thanksgiving gravy. Just click share to send or email recipes easily to both Chef it users and non-users.
